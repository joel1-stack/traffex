Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I pay for a booking?

Pay a KSh 5,000 deposit via M-Pesa STK Push on our booking page. Balance is due 7 days before travel.

How fast do you respond?

Within 15 minutes on WhatsApp during business hours. After hours, we reply first thing in the morning.

Can I book from abroad (diaspora)?

Yes! We accept PayPal for international payments. WhatsApp us and we will send a PayPal invoice.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 7 days before travel. Within 7 days, the deposit is non-refundable but transferable.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 6+ get 10% off. Corporate groups get custom pricing. WhatsApp us for a group quote.

How long does visa processing take?

We start your application within 24 hours of payment. Embassy processing: USA 2-4 weeks, UK 3-8 weeks.
