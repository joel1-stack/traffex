Who We Are

Traffex Tours & Travel is a registered Kenyan travel agency dedicated to making travel simple, affordable, and memorable. From the savannahs of the Masai Mara to the skyscrapers of Dubai, we handle every detail so you can focus on the experience.

We are not just a travel agency. We are your travel partner — from the moment you enquire to the moment you return home safely.
